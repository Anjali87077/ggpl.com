Principal's Message

Government Girls Polytechnic Lucknow aims to create a supportive and inclusive environment where talented students are encouraged to explore their potential to achieve their best in all aspects of technical life. Students will be engaged into creating opportunities that inspire to develop creativity, confidence and resilience to become independ and principled learners. The aim of this institute is to provide a broad curriculum and a range of opportunities for all our girls to achieve excellence in academic, creative, social, sporting and community and endeavours. Our students educated for this institute will succeed in the worldwith their skills, logical mindsets and technical qualities."Believe in yourself! Have faith in your abilities! Without a humble but reasonable confidence in your own powers you cannot be successful or happy."

Mr. S.K. Srivastava
